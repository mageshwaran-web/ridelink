Frequently Asked Questions
First, complete Driver Verification in your Profile. Then go to the Dashboard and tap "Offer Ride". Fill in your start location, destination, departure time, available seats, and your price per passenger. You can also click on the map to set exact pickup and drop points. Once posted, passengers can find and request your ride.
Tap "Find Ride" from the Dashboard or the Rides tab. Enter your pickup location and destination, then hit Search. You'll see a list of available rides sorted by match score. Tap any ride to see details and request it. Once the driver confirms, you'll see live tracking.
Once your ride is confirmed, you'll see a "Message" button on the waiting screen. In this demo version, messages are simulated. In the full version, you'll be able to call or message your driver directly through the app.
The SOS button appears during active rides (waiting, in-progress). Pressing it sends an emergency alert with your current GPS location to your registered Emergency Contact. Set up your emergency contact in Profile → Emergency Contact before riding.
After each completed ride, both the driver and passenger can rate each other on a 5-star scale with an optional comment. Ratings contribute to your overall score visible on your profile and ride cards. Higher-rated users are shown first in search results.
